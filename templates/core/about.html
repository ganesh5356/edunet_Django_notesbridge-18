About NotesBridge

NotesBridge is a collaborative learning platform designed to bridge the gap between seniors and juniors in academic institutions. Our mission is to create a supportive environment where knowledge flows freely, helping students achieve their academic goals through shared resources and peer guidance.

Founded with the vision of making education more accessible and collaborative, NotesBridge empowers seniors to share their valuable study materials while providing juniors with organized access to resources that enhance their learning experience.
